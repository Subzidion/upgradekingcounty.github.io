# Upgrade King County

We are a new working group that meets every other Thursday.
We are focused on digital equity, municipal broadband advocacy and connecting to people
across Washington State working on helping people to get online.

Check out the [draft zine](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1FChaeJ77dL04GbN1mQcVAHt-mT99-qzb/view?usp=sharing) we have created around Internet Access in Seattle.<br />
